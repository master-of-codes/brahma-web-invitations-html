Our Story

We are Brahma Web Invitations. We provide our customers with responsive, customizable, smooth scrolling, well-structured, clean, and user-friendly invitation card websites with transitions and animations.

Our mission is to make your special occasions even more memorable with beautifully designed digital invitations that reflect your unique style and personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

+ Are the invitations mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All our invitations are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. They look great on phones, tablets, and desktops, ensuring your guests can access them easily from any device.
+ Can I see a demo before purchasing?
Yes! Each invitation template has a live demo you can view. Click the "View Demo" button in the plans modal (or) click the Preview icon in the cards to see how your invitation will look and function.
+ What if I need changes after purchase?
We offer revision support for up to 7 days after purchase. Minor changes are included in the price. Feel free to Contact us if you need modifications.
+ Can I install this invitation?
Yes! You can install your invitation just like other apps and keep it permanently on your home screen. Anyone you share the invitation link with can also install it and access it easily. However, it is not actually an Android app.
Note: Installation Feature is only for the (Web Application) plan.
+ What kind of web apps do you provide?
We provide invitations as Progressive Web Apps (PWA). A PWA is a web application that behaves like a mobile app but runs in a browser.
+ What if I need changes after purchase?
We offer revision support for up to 7 days after purchase. Minor changes are included in the price. Feel free to Contact us if you need modifications.

+ How can I share this with everyone?

+ Can I add music?
Yes! You can add an MP3 music track to your invitation, and you can play or pause it anytime.
+ How long does it take for me to receive the invitation?
The Production time depends on the complexity of the design and customization. Simple modifications take maximum of 24 hours, while more complex ones may take 2 days. We always communicate timelines clearly during the ordering process.
